Rare facts about Biggest Boy Band on planet-BTS

Band name and the meaning behind it.

BTS stands for BangTan Soyeondan or Beyond The Scenes not 'Behind The Scenes'. BangTan Soyeondan in english means Bulletproof Boys' Scout.



Existence of the band.

The South Korean pop Band debuted on 12th June, 2013 under the BigHit Production label which is now known as HYBE label, one of the largest company in South Korea.

Members of the band.

There are 7 members in the band as follows:

  1. Kim Namjoon (RM)
  2. Kim Seokjin (Jin)
  3. Min Yoongi (Suga)
  4. Jung Hoseok (J-Hope)
  5. Park Jimin (Jimin)
  6. Kim Taehyung (V)
  7. Jeon Jungkook (Jungkook)
